The homepage should introduce the person behind the site before it starts behaving like an archive.

For this first version, the goal is simple:

- make the landing page feel personal
- keep the blog visible but not dominant
- leave room for future expansion without overwhelming the first screen

That balance is what makes the homepage useful instead of noisy.
